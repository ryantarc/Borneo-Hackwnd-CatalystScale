## Catalyst Scale

**Catalyst Scale** is an AI-powered platform designed to accelerate the growth of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs), particularly those in underserved or rural communities. Many small business owners have strong products and services but lack access to the resources, professional expertise, and funding opportunities needed to scale their businesses. Catalyst Scale aims to bridge this gap by creating a digital ecosystem that connects MSMEs with the people and tools they need to grow.

At its core, Catalyst Scale functions as a **professional and investor discovery platform**, allowing business owners to find and collaborate with professionals such as marketers, financial advisors, and consultants who can help improve and expand their services. In addition, the platform enables MSMEs to showcase their businesses to potential investors, opening new opportunities for funding and strategic partnerships.

To further support business owners, Catalyst Scale integrates an **AI-powered financial assistant** that simplifies financial management and decision-making. The AI helper can assist with organizing financial data, generating insights, and guiding entrepreneurs through essential financial tasks such as budgeting, expense tracking, and preparing business information that is ready to present to investors.

By combining **network access, professional expertise, and AI-driven financial guidance**, Catalyst Scale empowers MSMEs to overcome traditional barriers to growth. The platform aims to make business support, financial clarity, and investment opportunities more accessible, helping small businesses move from survival to sustainable growth.
